Linda's Ginger Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1.5 cups white sugar
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons corn syrup (dark)
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoon soda
  • 2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoon ginger
  • 2 teaspoon cloves
  • 2 teaspoon nutmeg
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add egg and corn syrup, beat well.
  4. Add dry ingredients and spices in and mix thoroughly.
  5. Roll the dough thin, and cut cookies.
  6. Place on greased cookie sheet and bake 5-10 minutes.
  7. Serve!
Additional Information

When cutting cookies, feel free to cut the cookies into seasonal shapes for the holidays!
