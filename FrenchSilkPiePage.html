French Silk Pie

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pounds of butter
  • Two large squares of baking chocolate
  • 1 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 9 1/2 ounces Cool Whip
  • Whipped cream
  • Pie crust of your choosing
Instructions
  1. Cream the butter and white sugar together.
  2. Add 2 eggs to butter and sugar mixture, one at a time.
  3. Take the other 2 eggs and beat for five minutes.
  4. Melt baking chocolate squares.
  5. Hdd melted chocolate and beaten eggs to mixture.
  6. Fold in Cool Whip into mixture.
  7. Pour the pie filling you just made into a ready to go pie crust.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 6 hours before serving.
  9. Garnish pie with whipped cream and chocolate curls.
  10. Serve!
Additional Information

For this pie, I highly recommend using a chocolate flavored crust. Yum!
