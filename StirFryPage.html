Steak and Bell Pepper Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef chuck roast or round (fat trimmed)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 grated ginger
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 1 cup green onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup red and green pepper, cut into 1” squares
  • 2 stalks of celery, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tomatoes, cut into wedges
Instructions
  1. With a very sharp knife, cut meat across grain into thin strips (1/8 inch thick).
  2. Combine soy sauce, garlic, and ginger in a bowl.
  3. Add sliced beef.
  4. Toss to coat and marinade beef. Set aside while preparing vegetables.
  5. Heat oil in large frying pan or wok.
  6. Add beef and toss over high heat until browned. If not tender, cover and simmer 30-40 minutes over low heat.
  7. Turn heat up to medium heat and add prepared vegetables (celery, bell peppers, green onion).
  8. Toss until tender crisp (about 10 minutes).
  9. Mix cornstarch to water in another bowl.
  10. Add cornstarch water mixture to pan.
  11. Stir and cook until thickened.
  12. Add tomatoes, heat through.
  13. Serve!
Additional Information

Serve over rice, chinese or regular noodles.
