Pizza Cups

Ingredients

  • 3/4 pound ground beef
  • 1 can (6 oz.) tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon Italian dressing
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 can (10 oz.) of refrigerated biscuits
  • 3/4 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Brown and drain beef.
  3. Stir in tomato paste, onion powder, salt and Italian dressing.
  4. Cook over low heat, stirring frequently.
  5. Place biscuits in greased muffin tins, pressing to cover bottom and sides.
  6. Hdd melted chocolate and beaten eggs to mixture.
  7. Spoon about 1/4 cup meat mixture into each biscuit shell and sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake for 12 minutes.
  9. Serve!
Additional Information

Add pepperoni or any veggies you would like on top before baking!
